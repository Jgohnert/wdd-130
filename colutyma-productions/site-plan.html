Purpose

[Hi, my name is Joanna Gohnert and I am a web developer looking to create a website for COLUTYMA productions. COLUTYMA is an independent film studio looking to make a name for themselves, which is exactly why I plan to help them by creating a website that will catalogue their movies with brief summaries and character bios to get the audience interested in supporting an aspiring team of talented creators. Wouldn’t it be easier for companies to be able to find talent through a website like this? Wouldn’t you want to be able to find and support small creators with just a few convenient clicks? I hope to do exactly that with the official COLUTYMA website. Please consider supporting me and the talented actors and producers behind COLUTYMA productions.]

Audience

[Families and movie fans that like supporting smaller film producers are the target audience for this website. This website will serve as a showcase for COLUTYMA productions' numerous works throughout the years, allowing the target audience to better understand the movies, characters, and actors that helped bring these films to life. Overall, we want the material we produce to entertain and delight people so that the platform may continue to develop for many years to come.]

Content

Home page

Main purpose of the COLUTYMA home page.
Those who visit our homepage will be looking for information about COLUTYMA productions first and foremost, which is why the primary purpose of the home page is to neatly organize the links to the information involving the cast, producers and movies on the other pages, and it will also serve to give the audience a general overview of what COLUTYMA productions is and the films they have produced since their childhood.
Home page. audience intrigue/interaction.
Another purpose of the homepage will provide the user with enough intrigue into the works of COLUTYMA productions that will garner more audiences to discover the passionate works of the producers and actors involved. One way the website will grab audiences’ interests is by showcases the movie posters and the names of each movie on a neat and organized layout. Links will also be provided in the movie titles, which will allow the user to click on the movie that most interested in so that they can read more about it on the second page. If the audience came to the homepage out of curiosity, the brief overview of the page should be enough to develop intrigue and the information to understand what it is that we hope to achieve by showcasing the various films of COLUTYMA productions.

Images for the Home page

[Movie List]

Main purpose of the COLUTYMA Movie List page.
There are two main purposes of the second page; The first is to provide a more in-depth spoiler free synopsis of each movie. The purpose of this is to familiarize the audience with the general story, as well as aesthetically pleasing postures that will help provide an overall feel for what the film is like and what to expect going in to it. The second purpose is to provide the users with further insights pertaining to the characters involved in each of their respected film, as well as their names.
Movie List page. audience intrigue/interaction.
If users of the COLUTYMA website wish to know more about the specifics of each movie, the second page is where all of that information will be. The audience will be able to read about a brief non spoiler synopsis of the film that they are interested in, which will help give them an idea as to what the movie is about. List of character names will also be provided as well, and an overall summary as to what the character’s purpose is in each respective film.

Images for the Page 2

[Actors and Producers]

Main purpose of the COLUTYMA Actors and producer’s page.
The third page will contain everything the audience would want to know about the actors and producers involved with COLUTYMA productions. For example, an image of the actor will be displayed, then underneath that will be their name and job descriptions, as well as their other accomplishments, hobbies and jobs that they have had in their life. This page will not include private information about the personal homelife of the actors and producers, it is only meant to showcase a little bit about their accomplishments and interests as a way to help the users get a better insight as to who they are and why they are passionate about COLUTYMA productions, and what it means to them to make these films as a whole.
Actors and producer’s page. audience intrigue/interaction.
As a way to prevent the audience from feeling blind as to who is behind the works of COLUTYMA productions, the third page is meant to give them an inside look into their motivations. This will let the users know why COLUTYMA was started and what they hope to achieve through their creativity within their films. It will give COLUTYMA a face and a friendly feeling when visiting the website, giving an authentic feel that will help the site stand out as a passion project for the talented and inspirational people behind COLUTYMA productions.
