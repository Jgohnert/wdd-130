About COLUTYMA Productions

COLUTYMA Productions was formed by Tyler Gohnert, Colby Schramel, Mathew Gohnert, and Luke Gohnert. Their goal was to express their love of cinema by creating their own films through COLUTYMA Productions.

The Gohnert siblings began filming when they were children in 2006. The first movie they filmed was called Fright, which was inspired by the classic 1984 comedy horror film, Gremlins. Since then, they've built a community of movie enthusiasts and actors that enjoy being a part of their projects, which culminated in the formation of COLUTYMA Productions in 2017.

COLUTYMA Productions has never profited from their work. They only create movies to give opportunity to aspiring creators, and to make something to share with their friends, family, and to foster a community with those who have similar passions for movies, and the films they create.
